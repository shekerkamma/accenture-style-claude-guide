# Win Strategy

## When To Use

Use after qualifying an opportunity and before the proposal team begins writing. A proposal written without a win strategy is a capabilities brochure. The win strategy answers the question the client is actually asking — "why you, not your competitor?" — and makes that answer the backbone of every section of the proposal.

## Consulting Approach

Win strategies are built from intelligence, not assumption. The three inputs are: what the client actually cares about (their business problem, their internal politics, their evaluation criteria), who the competitors are and what they will say, and what genuinely differentiates this firm from those competitors on this specific opportunity. Win themes are the 3–4 messages that, if believed by the client, make this firm the obvious choice. Every proposal section must reinforce at least one win theme.

## Workflow

1. Gather intelligence: client's strategic priorities, evaluation criteria, political landscape, incumbent relationship, budget constraints, and timeline pressures.
2. Identify the competitors: who is likely shortlisted, what will they say, what are their known weaknesses on this opportunity?
3. Define the client's hot buttons: the 2–3 things the client cares about most that will drive their selection decision.
4. Develop 3–4 win themes: the messages that position this firm as the best answer to the hot buttons and against the competitors.
5. Identify the vulnerabilities: where is this firm weaker than competitors, and how will those weaknesses be neutralised?
6. Validate the win strategy with the account team and pursuit lead before proposal writing begins.

## Quality Bar

- Win themes must answer "why us, not them on this specific opportunity" — generic capability statements are not win themes.
- Every competitor must be named — "other Big 4 firms" is not a competitive analysis.
- Hot buttons must come from client intelligence, not assumptions — document the source.
- Vulnerabilities must be honest — a win strategy that lists no vulnerabilities has not been completed.
